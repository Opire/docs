# What is MakeMyChange?

MakeMyChange is a developer rewards platform.

With MakeMyChange you can create rewards to your projects and increase your community, developers can do the issues and earning the rewards  

## How start?

First of all you need to install the App in your preferred platform (Github, GitLab or Bitbucket). This app must be installed at the organization level (if you have a personal account it can be installed at any option).

## How works?

The rewards are created writting commands in the issues.
